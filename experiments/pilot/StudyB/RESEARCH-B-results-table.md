## Reading it (interpretation guide — package thresholds only)
- **Primary question:** does the **BOLD-resolved** group beat the **SAFE** group on *gut-want* AND *buy-now* (Table 3)? If yes on both → the delight→confidence bet holds. If no on both → **business-critical falsifier** (bold doesn't sell; reconsider the promise).
- **Expert ≠ customer:** Table 2 shows where expert quality and customer desire diverge — keep every divergence visible; these rooms/proposals are the most informative.
- **Static caveat:** low "this is the one" rates are expected on static cards (inevitability may need the live session — Study C). Do not treat low "the one" alone as failure.
- **Price:** compare acceptable-price bands (their numbers) against our real price per room; flag any room where willingness-to-pay sits far below a viable price (a positioning issue independent of design).
