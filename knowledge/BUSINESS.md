# BUSINESS

Version: 0.1

## Purpose

This document defines the business strategy behind Latenca.

The goal is to build a company that delivers outstanding customer value
while creating a scalable and sustainable business.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Mission

Help people confidently design beautiful walls.

Revenue is a result of creating trust and reducing decision anxiety.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Customer Segments

## Primary

-   Homeowners
-   Apartment owners
-   First-time decorators
-   Families renovating interiors

## Secondary

-   Interior designers
-   Airbnb hosts
-   Hotels
-   Offices
-   Restaurants
-   Property developers

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Value Proposition

Latenca helps customers:

-   Discover inspiration
-   Build complete wall projects
-   Receive AI design guidance
-   Buy with confidence

Unlike traditional art stores, Latenca sells confidence---not just
artwork.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Business Principles

1.  Trust before sales.
2.  Long-term customer relationships.
3.  Mobile-first commerce.
4.  AI creates value before asking for payment.
5.  Continuous product improvement.
