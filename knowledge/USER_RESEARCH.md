# USER_RESEARCH

Version: 0.1

## Purpose

This document is the single source of truth about Latenca users.

It documents what users actually need, fear, expect and how they make
purchasing decisions.

The goal is to build the product around people rather than assumptions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Customer Segments

## Primary Segment

People decorating their own home.

Typical situations:

-   New apartment
-   New house
-   Renovation
-   Empty wall after moving
-   Replacing old decorations

Characteristics

-   Visits once or twice
-   Limited design knowledge
-   Wants confidence
-   Wants quick decisions

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Secondary Segment

People decorating multiple spaces.

Examples

-   Interior designers
-   Airbnb owners
-   Hotels
-   Offices
-   Restaurants

Characteristics

-   Returns frequently
-   Needs efficiency
-   Values reusable projects
-   Often buys multiple artworks

-   Find artwork matching my room

## Emotional

-   Feel proud of my home

## Social

-   Impress visitors

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Decision Journey

1.  Notice empty wall
2.  Search for inspiration
3.  Explore styles
4.  Compare options
5.  Seek reassurance
6.  Purchase
7.  Evaluate after installation

Latenca should reduce uncertainty at every step.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Pain Points

-   Too many choices
-   Fear of buying the wrong artwork
-   Hard to imagine the final wall
-   Unsure about sizes
-   Unsure about layouts
-   Unsure whether artworks match

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# User Questions

Examples:

-   Is this too small?
-   Should I buy one picture or a gallery wall?
-   Does this fit my sofa?
-   Should I add frames?
-   Will these colors work together?

These questions should eventually be answered by AI.

# Success Metrics

A successful experience means:

-   Higher satisfaction
-   Higher average order value without pressure
